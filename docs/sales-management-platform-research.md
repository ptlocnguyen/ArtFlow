# Nghien cuu cac nen tang quan ly ban hang noi bat

Ngay cap nhat: 2026-06-18

## Muc tieu

Tai lieu nay tong hop nhung diem dang hoc tu cac nen tang CRM, POS va quan ly ban hang pho bien de lam co so xay dung san pham ArtFlow.

## Nhom san pham tham khao

### 1. Salesforce Sales Cloud

- Dinh vi: CRM doanh nghiep, manh ve pipeline, du bao, automation va AI.
- Diem manh: he sinh thai lon, kha nang tuy bien cao, bao cao/cham diem co hoi, tich hop Slack va cong cu doanh nghiep.
- Diem can hoc: thiet ke pipeline ro rang theo giai doan, dashboard KPI cho quan ly, automation cho tac vu lap lai.
- Can tranh: phuc tap voi doanh nghiep nho neu dua qua nhieu cau hinh ngay tu dau.

### 2. HubSpot Sales Hub

- Dinh vi: CRM de tiep can cho SME, co goi mien phi, lien ket sales/marketing/service.
- Diem manh: onboarding de, live chat, meeting scheduler, email sequence, deal tracking, marketing CRM lien thong.
- Diem can hoc: trai nghiem nguoi moi rat mem, co free tier, luong thao tac ban hang tu lien he -> deal -> cham soc khach rat tu nhien.
- Can tranh: tinh nang nang cao co the nam o cac goi cao, chi phi tang khi doi ngu mo rong.

### 3. Zoho CRM

- Dinh vi: CRM gia tot cho doanh nghiep nho/vua, co he sinh thai Zoho rong.
- Diem manh: lead/deal/workflow/report/mobile app, free cho toi 3 user, tich hop Zoho Books/Desk va ben thu ba.
- Diem can hoc: cho phep bat dau gon nhe nhung van co duong mo rong len automation, AI, bao cao va tich hop.
- Can tranh: tuy bien qua sau co the lam nguoi dung moi bi roi.

### 4. Pipedrive

- Dinh vi: CRM tap trung ban hang, pipeline truc quan.
- Diem manh: keo-tha deal theo pipeline, activity-based selling, de dieu huong, mobile tot, nhieu tich hop.
- Diem can hoc: giao dien pipeline nen la man hinh trung tam; moi co hoi ban hang phai co hanh dong tiep theo ro rang.
- Can tranh: thieu marketing automation sau neu muon lam all-in-one.

### 5. Shopify POS

- Dinh vi: POS cho ban le da kenh, dac biet manh khi ket hop ecommerce.
- Diem manh: dong bo online/offline, quan ly ton kho da dia diem, staff permissions, retail reporting, pickup/fulfillment.
- Diem can hoc: mot ma san pham/khach hang/don hang dung chung cho online va cua hang vat ly.
- Can tranh: chi phi POS Pro va he sinh thai co the cao voi shop nho.

### 6. Square POS

- Dinh vi: POS de bat dau cho doanh nghiep nho, linh hoat theo nganh.
- Diem manh: POS co goi mien phi, thanh toan tich hop, report ban hang, loyalty, marketing, staff management.
- Diem can hoc: checkout phai rat nhanh; bao cao phai de doc ngay ca voi chu shop khong chuyen ky thuat.
- Can tranh: phu thuoc payment ecosystem va phi giao dich.

### 7. Lightspeed Retail

- Dinh vi: POS ban le co ton kho phuc tap, da dia diem, chuoi cua hang.
- Diem manh: inventory nang cao, product variants, vendor/supplier, purchase orders, low-stock alerts, reporting sau.
- Diem can hoc: module kho can co canh bao ton thap, nhap hang, nha cung cap, bien the san pham, bao cao hang cham luan chuyen.
- Can tranh: chi phi va do phuc tap cao hon cac giai phap nho.

### 8. Odoo POS / Sales / Inventory

- Dinh vi: bo ung dung ERP mo rong, ket noi CRM, ban hang, POS, kho, ke toan, ecommerce.
- Diem manh: tich hop lien thong giua cac module, POS chay tren trinh duyet, ho tro offline tam thoi, dong bo ton kho realtime.
- Diem can hoc: kien truc module hoa; du lieu ban hang nen chay thong suot sang kho, ke toan va bao cao.
- Can tranh: trien khai day du ERP co the nang voi doi ngu nho.

### 9. KiotViet

- Dinh vi: phan mem quan ly ban hang pho bien tai Viet Nam, nhieu nganh hang.
- Diem manh: quan ly hang hoa, nhan su, bao cao doanh thu, ban hang da kenh, dung thu mien phi, chi phi theo ngay/thang.
- Diem can hoc: ngon ngu san pham gan voi chu shop Viet Nam; quy trinh phai don gian, nhanh, de dung tren dien thoai.
- Can tranh: can thiet ke linh hoat cho tung nganh nhung khong lam he thong roi rac.

### 10. Sapo

- Dinh vi: quan ly ban hang online den offline, website, san TMĐT, social commerce.
- Diem manh: quan ly kho realtime, ban tai cua hang, MXH, san TMĐT, website, chuong trinh khuyen mai/tich diem da kenh.
- Diem can hoc: omnichannel cho thi truong Viet Nam nen uu tien Facebook, san TMĐT, chat/inbox va tao don tu hoi thoai.
- Can tranh: goi tinh nang qua nhieu can duoc dong goi ro de nguoi mua de chon.

## Mau so chung dang hoc

1. Dashboard dau ngay
   - Doanh thu hom nay, don moi, loi nhuan uoc tinh, san pham sap het, cong viec can lam.

2. Pipeline hoac trang thai don hang
   - CRM thi dung pipeline lead/deal.
   - Ban le/POS thi dung trang thai don: moi, dang xu ly, da giao, hoan/doi tra.

3. Ton kho realtime
   - Moi giao dich ban/nhap/tra hang cap nhat ton kho ngay.
   - Co canh bao hang sap het, hang ton lau, hang ban chay.

4. Ban hang nhanh
   - Tim san pham nhanh, quet ma vach, chiet khau, nhieu phuong thuc thanh toan, in/xuat hoa don.

5. Khach hang la tai san
   - Luu lich su mua, tong chi tieu, nhom khach, ghi chu, diem thuong, cong no neu co.

6. Bao cao de ra quyet dinh
   - Doanh thu theo ngay/thang/kenh/nhan vien/san pham.
   - Loi nhuan, gia von, ti le chuyen doi, san pham ban cham.

7. Da kenh
   - Cua hang, website, Facebook/chat, san TMĐT, POS mobile can gom ve mot noi.

8. Phan quyen nhan vien
   - Chu shop, quan ly, thu ngan, nhan vien kho, cham soc khach co quyen khac nhau.

9. Automation vua du
   - Nhac follow-up khach hang, tu tao viec sau khi co lead, canh bao ton kho, thong bao don loi.

10. Mobile-first cho chu shop
   - Chu shop thuong xem bao cao, duyet don, kiem ton va theo doi doanh thu tren dien thoai.

## Goi y MVP cho ArtFlow

### MVP 1: Loi ban hang va kho

- Dang nhap va phan quyen co ban.
- Quan ly san pham, SKU, bien the, gia ban, gia von, ton kho.
- Ban hang/POS nhanh.
- Don hang va thanh toan.
- Khach hang va lich su mua.
- Dashboard doanh thu/ngay, don hang, ton kho thap.

### MVP 2: Da kenh va van hanh

- Quan ly kenh ban: cua hang, online, social, san TMĐT.
- Nhap hang, nha cung cap, phieu kho.
- Doi tra hang.
- Quan ly nhan vien, ca lam, doanh so theo nhan vien.
- Bao cao loi nhuan va san pham ban chay/ban cham.

### MVP 3: CRM va tu dong hoa

- Lead/deal pipeline cho ban hang tu van.
- Nhac viec, lich hen, email/tin nhan mau.
- Nhom khach hang, diem thuong, voucher.
- Automation canh bao ton kho, cham soc lai khach cu.
- API/tich hop ben thu ba.

## Dieu nen uu tien trong UX

- Man hinh dau tien nen la Dashboard van hanh, khong phai landing page.
- Sidebar ro rang: Tong quan, Ban hang, Don hang, San pham, Kho, Khach hang, Bao cao, Cau hinh.
- Tac vu thu ngan phai it buoc nhat co the.
- Bang du lieu can co tim kiem, loc, sap xep, import/export Excel.
- Moi trang nen co CTA chinh ro: Tao don, Them san pham, Nhap hang, Tao khach.
- Du lieu rong can co empty state huong dan hanh dong tiep theo.

## Loi the can tao neu xay cho thi truong Viet Nam

- Ho tro VND, dia chi Viet Nam, so dien thoai, Zalo/Facebook, COD.
- Tao don tu chat/social.
- Ket noi van chuyen noi dia.
- Import Excel de chuyen tu so sach cu.
- Giao dien tieng Viet tu nhien, dung ngon ngu cua chu shop.
- Goi gia de hieu theo quy mo: ca nhan, shop nho, shop da kenh, chuoi cua hang.
